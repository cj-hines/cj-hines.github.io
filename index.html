Hiya I'm CJ

Born in 2002 in Manila, Philippines and raised in sunny San Diego. I'm passionate about building and designing technology for a sustainable, equitable, and accessible tomorrow. I recently earned both my B.A in Computer Science and B.A in Economics at Berkeley with a year of coursework completed at King's College London and the University of Cambridge, Pembroke College. At Berkeley, I was involved in the Society of Women Engineers, The Berkeley Forum, research withing the UCB Division of Computing, Data Science, and Society, and the Pilipinx Association of Scientists, Architects, and Engineers. At King's College London, I was part of the Muay Thai team. Most of my professional background has been in the renewable energy space as well as the startup space, working as a software developer at Nextracker and engineer at Energport during my undergrad. Beyond ice hockey, I'm also a lover of medieval and war history, development economics, HCI, machine learning, music production, and yoga.
